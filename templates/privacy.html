Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service to you. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorized access. Your financial data is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers do not have access to your financial data beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We take privacy seriously and will respond to all inquiries promptly.
